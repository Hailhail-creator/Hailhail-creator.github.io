About Me

Hi! I'm Jordan Phillips, a Junior Unity Game Developer with a passion for creating immersive and interactive game experiences. I specialize in using Unity for both 2D and 3D game development, and I complement my technical skills with 3D modeling and animation using Blender. My journey into game development has been shaped by hands-on projects, creative exploration, and formal training — most recently through the SkillsCity Game Development Bootcamp.

During my time at SkillsCity, I gained practical experience in designing and building games from the ground up. The bootcamp provided me with in-depth exposure to Unity's powerful toolset, including C# scripting, physics, animation systems, UI/UX design, and optimization techniques. I also worked on team-based projects that simulated real-world game development environments, sharpening my collaboration, problem-solving, and agile workflow skills.

In parallel, I used Blender to create custom 3D assets, environments, and basic animations — skills that I now regularly integrate into my Unity projects. Being able to produce and animate my own models has allowed me to bring unique, stylized elements into my games, giving them a personal creative touch.

This portfolio highlights some of the projects I've developed so far, including solo builds and collaborative efforts from the bootcamp. Each one showcases not only the technical and creative skills I've developed, but also my commitment to learning, improving, and pushing the boundaries of what I can create as a game developer.

Thank you for visiting — I'm excited to share my work and look forward to connecting with others who are as passionate about game development as I am!
